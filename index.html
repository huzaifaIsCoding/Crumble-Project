Spreading Happiness One Cookie At A Time

Who would’ve thought that a dorm room startup would become one of Pakistan's fastest-growing cookie businesses?

It all started in the dorm rooms of GIKI Islamabad, where a passion for baking turned into something bigger. In the beginning, it was nothing more than a humble countertop display on the university premises. Within no time, the soon-to-be-famous Crumble cookies started getting the attention they deserved, especially from students, teachers, and staff at GIKI. Initially, we operated from home, where everybody pitched in to make Crumble what it is today.

As the demand for our scrumptious cookies grew, we decided it was time to transform our little cookie bakery into a business startup. The idea was daunting at the time, but we took a leap of faith and began our venture in the bustling commercial center at Chaklala Scheme 3, Rawalpindi. That was the moment Crumble was born—officially!

Crumble Pakistan now caters to a wide sweet-tooth customer base with its nationwide delivery. Warmly, Crumble Pakistan
